In manual mode, this is auto-calculated and cannot be edited.

Strategy Explanations:

  • Manual Mode: Directly applies the amount entered in the "How much you want to pay?" field for each individual loan.
    Note: Once a loan is paid off, its payment is NOT reallocated. Your total out-of-pocket budget will naturally decrease over time.
  • Equally Split Extra Across Loans: Pays all base planned payments first, then takes the remainder of your Total Budget and distributes it evenly across all active loans.
  • Highest Monthly Interest First: Pays all base planned payments first, then funnels all remaining cash into the loan generating the largest absolute interest charge (₹) that month.
  • Highest ROI First (Avalanche): Pays all base planned payments first, then funnels all remaining cash into the loan with the highest percentage interest rate (%). This mathematically guarantees the absolute minimum lifetime interest.
  • Note on Automated Modes: Once a loan is paid off, its payment is aggressively reallocated to the remaining loans until you are completely debt-free. You will pay the same total amount every month until the end.
